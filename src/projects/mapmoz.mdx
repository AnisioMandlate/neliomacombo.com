title: "MAPMOZ"
description: "MapMoz is a collaborative mapping program aimed at identifying areas vulnerable to natural disasters. In the MapMoz program, developers, designers, architects, civil engineers, and communities work together to help map neighborhoods prone to flooding, contributing to the reduction of natural disaster risks and the development of solutions to protect new infrastructure. MapMoz is an initiative of the World Bank in partnership with MozDevz, the Eduardo Mondlane University (UEM), and the Municipal Councils of Maputo and Matola."
